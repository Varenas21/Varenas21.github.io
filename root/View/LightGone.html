LightGone

Neumont Collaborative Project

January - March 2024

Challenge

Design and develop an engaging and fully functional game, "LightGone", within a tight deadline of 10 weeks. This involved intense collaboration, rapid prototyping, and iterative development to ensure we could deliver a polished product on time. Our team had to manage all aspeccts of game development, from concept and design to coding and testing, all while mainting high standards of quality and creativity.

Solution

To meet the challenge of developing "LightGone" within the 10-week timeframe, our team employed a structured and agile approach. We broke down the project into manageable phases, including planning, prototyping, development and testing.


  • Effective collaboration : Utilized collaboration tools such as GitHub and Trello and held regular meetings to ensure clear communication and alignment within the team. This allowed us to quickly adress any issues and make necessary changes.
  • Prototyping : Created quick prototypes to test our ideas and gather feedback early in the development process. This iterative approach helped us refine gameply mechanics and design elements efficiently.
  • Task Priorization: By prioritizing tasks based on their impact on the overall project and deadlines, we ensured that critical features were developed first, allowing us to focus on polishing and enhancing the game in the later stages.
