Challenge

Design and develop an engaging and interactive software project, "NotABurger", as a part of a projects class at Neumont College of Computer Science. The site was to serve as an Alternative Reality Game (ARG) for a fictional burger place that uses humans as their meat. Our team needed to create an immersive user experience while ensuring the site's functionality and security. The project involved various aspects of web development, including backend logic, frontend design, user interaction, and database management, all within a tight deadline.

Solution

To meet the challenge of developing "NotABurger", our team employed a structured and agile approach. We broke down the project into manageable phases, including planning, prototyping, development and testing.


  • Effective Collaboration: Utilized collaboration tools such as GitHub and Trello and held regular meetings to ensure clear communication and alignment within the team. This allowed us to quickly address any issues and make necessary changes.
  • Immersive Design: Focused on creating an engaging and eerie atmosphere that fit the ARG theme. This included designing visually compelling graphics through AI and a user interface that enhanced the storytelling aspect of the game.
  • Dynamic Content: Developed dynamic content and interactive elements using ASP.NET Core to keep users engaged. This involved creating puzzles, clues, and narrative elements that unfolded as users navigated the site.
